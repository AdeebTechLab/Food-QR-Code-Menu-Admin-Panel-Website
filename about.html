The Story of Hot N Spicy

In A Relationship With Good Food

A Legacy Born from Passion, Served with Love

Some dreams are born not in boardrooms, but in the heart. Saeed Khan was a man with a restless soul and an undying love for food. After trying his luck in different jobs and businesses, he realized his true calling — to create a place where food wasn't just cooked, but crafted with love, passion, and an obsession for perfection.

In 1999, at Khadda Market, Karachi, with just a handful of equipment and a fire in his heart, he set up a small kitchen. It wasn't grand, but it had something far more valuable: a dream to bring people closer through food that spoke to the soul. He didn't just hire chefs; he built a family of culinary artists, masters of the grill, kings of the stove, and wizards of flavor. They worked tirelessly using only the freshest ingredients, tweaking and refining recipes until every bite was just right.

And then, magic happened.

Word spread faster than wildfire. At Hot N Spicy, we truly believe in being "In a Relationship with Good Food." Hot N Spicy wasn't just a restaurant; it became an experience. The aroma of sizzling grills, the sight of chefs working their magic, and the taste of food that left people speechless — it was unlike anything Karachi had seen before. Customers lined up in their cars, transforming the streets into a giant, open-air dining hall. By midnight, exhausted servers would have no choice but to call it a night. But the cravings never stopped — people even offered hefty tips just to get their orders in before closing time!

Saeed Khan's journey was one of passion, resilience, and an unbreakable bond with food lovers. In 2005, at just 42 years old, he passed away, leaving behind a legacy that would never fade. His dream, his flavors, and his spirit live on in every plate served at Hot N Spicy.

Today, Hot N Spicy proudly continues that legacy under the leadership of Raza Khan, who serves as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO). Following in the footsteps of his late father, founder Saeed Khan, he remains committed to preserving the brand's core values while driving innovation, maintaining exceptional quality, enhancing customer satisfaction, and expanding Hot N Spicy's presence across Pakistan.

Timeline

1999

Hot N Spicy started at Khadda Market, Karachi.

2004

The legendary Garlic Mayo Roll was born — a game-changer that remains our best-seller to this day.

2006

Karachi's love for Hot N Spicy grew, and we expanded, opening our second outlet, bringing more people into our world of flavors.

2022

We took a bold step forward, opening a dining restaurant in Islamabad, marking our presence in two of Pakistan's biggest cities.

From a small, passionate kitchen to a household name across Pakistan, Hot N Spicy isn't just about food — it's about memories, emotions, and the unbreakable bond between people and great flavors.
